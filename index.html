WHO
WE ARE?

We are an eco-friendly space for leisure and recreation for people who are fond of a healthy lifestyle. We are not just like a restaurant nor supermarket of vegan food and superfoods. We are happy to unite people with similar outlooks on life. Our visitors are people of all ages. We are also dog-friendly. In our place you all can take a break from the hustle and bustle of a big city and learn something new about your body and mind.

Every Saturday we have lectures about healthy lifestyle. We take self-care as a kind of philosophy. Welcome to our trainings, meditations, mantra readings, mandala painting, Hindi lessons, lectures from a nutritionist, psychologist and yoga instructor from India.

What
about food?

Right, it must be said about food.
Our menu mainly consists of fresh and cooked fruits and vegetables. We do not serve meat dishes. French fries are always made from sweet potatoes and vegetables served with tomato sauce. Instead of frying, we bake fries in the oven. We use wooden dishes in our place. There is also a vegan food market in our place.
We have a "happy hour" promotion - from 12:00 to 3:00 there is a 20% discount on meal and drinks.
Moreover, we prepare a cup of tea for the first visitor absolutely free every day.

IT'S
tea time!

What could be better than a good cup of tea? Our experts say that a good plantation-harvested tea, properly brewed, will not only keep you warm and make an excellent pair for sweets, but will also relieve headaches, improve your metabolism, and even stop the growth of cancer cells. Every Wednesday we host a tea party with a fixed fee at the entrance - only 12$.
